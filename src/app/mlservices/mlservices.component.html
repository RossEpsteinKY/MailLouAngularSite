NCOA Reporting

NCOA, National Change of Address is a process that matches a file of names and addresses to the United States Postal Service® database of 160+ million records of individual, family and business moves.

Clean data and Address Standardization

Our custom software will fix and correct such items as street addresses, standardization of street prefixes and suffixes, and identification and flagging of undeliverable addresses. We will also update addresses by matching them to the USPS National Change of Address database. Did you know that 12% of the average customers database is incorrect? A mail piece that has been incorrectly addressed can be delayed, or even worse, may be discarded. The USPS offers postage discounts to mailers that use software to standardize their mail list. We use this software at Mail Louisville, Inc. to eliminate duplicate addresses, improve delivery time and reduce postage costs.

Presorting

The benefits of “Co-mingling” mail usually are weighted towards a presort house and there is typically a better option. Although these discounts are available, it may be difficult for the average mailer to comply with the complex regulations. Mail Louisville, Inc. presorts thousands of pieces of mail each day. How do we save our customers money? Performing this presorting up front eliminates the need for the Post Office to do so. As a result, we can pass along up to a 30% discount on postage to you. We do not profit from postage and our skilled staff can guarantee that you will get the lowest possible rate.
