One of every five households move each year. Companies are also constantly on the move. As a result, you risk sending mail pieces which the Post Office cannot deliver. This un-deliverable mail can quickly exhaust your budget. Lastly, undeliverable standard mail is recycled by the Post Office.
N.C.O.A. (National Change of Address) is part of our sophisticated software and is available to you as needed.
National Change of Address compares names and addresses in your database with the latest address changes files with the USPS and uses this information to update your list. Using NCOA allows you to track valuable customers and prospects as they move. This process will dramatically reduce un-deliverables and reduce printing and mailing costs.
