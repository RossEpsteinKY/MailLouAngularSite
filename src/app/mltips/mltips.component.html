Mailing Tips

Pre-Mailing Check List:


  • Select a target market.
  • Provide Print Ready Artwork to Mail Louisville, or, use one of our In-House Designers
  • Choose the format, color and type of paper for your mail piece.
  • Mail Louisville, Inc. will help you obtain a targeted mail list based on required demographics and merge/purge your list to eliminate costly duplicates.
  • Clean your database of undeliverable addresses. We will consult you on the best method of address hygiene which will allow you to reduce over-printing.
  • To take advantage of the least expensive postage rates, Mail Louisville, Inc. will advise you on the best mail piece design and barcode placement to meet USPS requirements.
  • Select the class of delivery that best meets your needs and budget (1st Class, Standard Mail, Non Profit)
  • Choose a method of applying postage; (permit, postage meter or stamps). Permits can be expensive, however, we offer free use of our permits.
  • Mail Louisville, Inc. will postal code the database along with performing a merge/purge. We will contact you with approximate postage and total cost of the job prior to mailing.
  • Place your print order and request delivery expectations.
  • Arrange for payment. We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, check or wire prior to mailing.
  • In almost all circumstances, your mailing will receive 48 hour service after completion of printing.
